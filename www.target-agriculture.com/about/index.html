We process raw cashew nuts into edible cashew kernels for bulk export to the United States in 11.35 kg (25 lbs.) plastic pouches, which is vacuumed and flushed with CO2, then sealed and put in cartons for export.

Our project is to set up a Cashew Nut Processing Plant with an initial 16 tons per day capacity in year 1 and 32 tons per day from year 2 upwards â€“ employing minimum of 275 persons per shift with 80% of them women.
It entails buying raw cashew nuts from identified & established raw cashew nuts farmers from Oyo, Kwara and Kogi States. The raw nuts are then processed into edible cashew kernels that meet the USA Food & Drug Administration standard.
The finished products are parked in flexible plastic pouches, vacuumed, flushed with carbon dioxide gas (CO2), sealed & parked in cartoons for export to USA .

Economic incentives of our undertaking are listed below:

  • Tax breaks from Nigerian Investment Promotion Centre
  • Export development fund from NEPC
  • Export Expansion Grant under review
  • 5-year NEPC cashew strategy and collaboration with USAID-NEXTT adopted in October 2014



(C.E.L.) Cashew Processing Factory Project

The African cashew industry could represent the single biggest opportunity for the international cashew market over the next two decades. With growing domestic consumption in Asia (particularly in India and China), the industry will need increased supplies, and Africa represents the greatest potential for additional cashew product. However, the African industry will need long-term investment and development: a complete value-added infrastructure from growing and harvesting to processing and marketing.

C.E.L. PLANS A PROCESSING PLANT IN LEKKI FREE ZONE, WITH A CAPACITY OF 16TONS PER DAY IN YEAR1, GROWING TO 32 TONS/DAY IN YEAR 2. THIS CAPACITY IS BASED ON A CONFIRMED 5 YEAR PURCHASE CONTRACT FROM OUR US BUYER SUNRISE COMMODITIES INC OF NEW JERSEY, USA.

THE PLANT WILL GENERATE EMPLOYMENT FOR 301 WORKERS IN YEAR1, GROWING TO 568 EMPLOYEES IN YEAR2, 80% OF THE WORKERS ARE WOMEN.
THE FOREIGN EXCHANGE INFLOW EXPECTED IS USD 360,000 PER MONTH IN YEAR1, GROWING TO USD 720,000 PER MONTH IN YEAR2. BASED ON CURRENT PRICES. THE ONLY RAW MATERIAL INPUT REQUIRED, IS 100% AVAILABLE LOCALLY.
WITH THE PROCESSING KNOW HOW TO MEET THE AMERICAN FOOD INDUSTRY STANDARD(AFI) WE ARE REGISTERED WITH THE US FOOD AND DRUG ADMINISTRATION (FDA) REGISTRATION NUMBER: 16156667880 .
