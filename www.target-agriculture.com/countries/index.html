WORLD CASHEW MARKET

To fully understand the dynamics of the World cashew market, it has to be looked at as two (2) markets in one, because of the clear distinctions in the supply and demand characteristics that make up the World cashew market.
There is the market for the Raw Cashew Nuts, and the market for the Processed Cashew Kernels.

RAW CASHEW NUTS MARKETS

The Raw Cashew nuts, Anarcadium Occidental, is a tree nut from the ever green Cashew tree that grows mostly in the tropical areas. The fruiting season however differs depending on whether the producing Country is above the equator (0°) or below.
For the Nations above the equator, the season runs from February to July, while Countries below the equator have their season from August to December.

PROCESSED CASHEW KERNEL MARKET

The processed cashew kernel market is radically different from the raw cashew nut market because the processed kernel is a high priced luxury snack and the demand for it is highly correlated to the level of disposable income. The supply of processed kernels is very skewed, as three countries; India, Vietnam and Brazil control about 95% of the market.

Elsewhere on the island, the wet, warm climate makes Sri Lanka the perfect place to grow a wide variety of tropical fruits, like coconuts, pineapples, mangos, and many more.

The consumption of the nut, i.e. demand for it, is more broadly spread out. The US consumes 55% of the Worlds’ output, Europe - 20%, Japan – 10%, Asia – 10%, China and India have the fastest consumption growth rate as the disposable income in both countries are growing. The general trend is for continued growth in the demand for cashew kernels, especially due to the increased awareness of its health benefits. The market dynamics are changing as shown in the quotation from Red River Foods, a major cashew marketer in the US.

CASHEW PROCESSING

Africa with the production of 40% of the Worlds’ raw cashew nuts, but a paltry 5% of the Worlds’ processed kernels, has a huge untapped potential in developing its’ cashew processing industry.
Cashew processing, simply put, is the process of converting the raw cashew nuts into edible kernels.

A good processed cashew kernel, is the one that is presented after the conversion, in the EXACT – SHAPE, COLOUR AND ITS’ NATURAL SMELL.

  • Don’t change the shape
  • Don’t change the colour – (White)
  • Don’t introduce any extraneous smell during the conversion from raw cashew nuts.

Year 1: Installed processing capacity is 16tons of raw nuts per day.

  • Raw nut requirement for the year, working 5 days/week, and 40 weeks/year is 16tons x 200 working days= 3,200 tons of raw cashew nuts.
  • Cost of raw nuts delivered to factory warehouse from farm gate is $750/ton.
  • Conversion rate, raw nut to kernel is 5.4tons raw nut to 1ton of processed kernel (5.4:1)

Shells generated in the process :

The selling yield of raw cashew nuts is approximately 75% by weight. So by processing 16tons/day, we generate 12 tons of shell as waste. The Cashew Nut Shell Liquid (CNSL) we intend to extract from the shell is 15% of the shell by weight.
So we generate 1.8 tons of CNSL/day (12tons x .15). Working 200days/year, this gives us 1.8 x 200= 360tons of CNSL annually.
